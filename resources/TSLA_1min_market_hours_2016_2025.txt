Content of the dataset TSLA_1min_market_hours_2016_2025.csv:

timestamp - timestamp of the 1-minute interval (market hours)

symbol - stock ticker symbol (TSLA)

open - opening price at the start of the minute

high - highest price during the minute

low - lowest price during the minute

close - closing price at the end of the minute

volume - total number of shares traded during the minute

trade_count - number of trades executed during the minute

vwap - The average price of the stock during the minute, 
weighted by trading volume. Prices with more shares traded have a bigger impact on this value.
